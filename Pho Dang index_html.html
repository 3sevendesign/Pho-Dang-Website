Our Story

Cooked with the
same hands that
raised us

Pho Dang was born from a mother's kitchen — a place where the broth simmered for hours, the herbs were always fresh, and the door was always open. We brought that spirit with us to Winooski, Vermont, and we've never let it go.

Everything on our menu is prepared the way it's meant to be: slow, patient, and with intention. Our pho broth is made fresh every single morning. Our spring rolls are hand-rolled. Our family runs this kitchen the same way it ran ours — together.

— The Dang Family
"Bringing to you the best Vietnamese food in town — made with love, just like home."
